Refund Policy

When a payment of fee is made to LegalRaasta, the fees paid in advance is retained by LegalRaasta in a client account. LegalRaasta will earn the fees upon working on a client’s matter. During an engagement, LegalRaasta earns fee at different rates and different times depending on the completion of various milestones (e.g. obtaining DIN, Filing of forms,assigning relationship manager, etc.). Refund cannot be provided for earned fee because resources and man hours spent on delivering the service are non-returnable in nature. Inshort, processing fees, payment gateway charges, admin fees, and cost of resources for the work done till date will be deducted. Further, we can’t refund or credit any money paid to government entities, such as filing fees or taxes, or to other third parties with a role in processing your order. Under any circumstance, LegalRaasta shall be liable to refund only upto the fee paid by the client.


Refund Request

Refund request can be initiated through refund@legalraasta.com. All refund requests must be made within 6 days of purchase. We will process your request within 15 to 25 business days of receiving all the information required for processing refund like reason for refund, bank details for processing request, etc.


Change of Service

If you want to change the service you ordered for a different one, you must request this change of service within 6 days of purchase. The purchase price of the original service, less any earned fee and money paid to government entities, such as filing fees or taxes, or to other third parties with a role in processing your order, will be credited to your LegalRaasta account. You can use the balance credit for any other LegalRaasta service.

We cannot guarantee the results or outcome of your particular procedure. For instance, the government may reject a trademark application for legal reasons beyond the scope of LegalRaasta service.

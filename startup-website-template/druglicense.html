Drug License (Retail/Whoesale) Registration


Licensing is obligatory to do business in Drugs & Cosmetics in India. The arrangements for this have been made in the Drugs & Cosmetics Act, 1940 which applies to entire India. It is an Act to combine all the laws identifying with drugs & cosmetics, which set up the Drugs Control Department. In 1964, the Act was amended to incorporate Ayurvedic & Unani drugs. Anyway, a selected business entity may require distinctive forms of drug licenses based on their enterprise; you may get in touch with LegalRaasta for consultancy on it.

In case a business is operating in more than two states, it needs to get a drug license in every state in which business is being continued. Drug license is area explicit. On the off chance that medications are sold or supplied available to be pu0chased at more than one spot, the application shall be made and license shall be issued, in regards to each such spot exception of migrant vendors.
